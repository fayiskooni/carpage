ATHLETIC COUPÉ SILHOUETTE

A vehicle with sculpted lines and a smoothly sloping coupé roof, combined with exclusive light-alloy wheels and aerodynamic M exterior mirrors, radiates concentrated sporting energy. The elongated silhouette culminates in a striking rear end that embodies restrained power.

LED HEADLIGHTS AND TAIL LIGHTS

In the rear lights, as in the headlights, all lighting functions are provided exclusively by LEDs. Their elongated L-shaped form and the design with continuous light lines ensure a smooth transition at the junction with the trunk lid and emphasize the elegance of the rear of the vehicle’s body.

M COMPETITION ENGINE

The 3.0-liter M TwinPower Turbo engine combines the best of two worlds: the ability to rev high—up to 7,600 rpm—and an impressive torque output of 550 Nm. Twin-turbo fuel injection and precise tuning ensure explosive acceleration and confident, powerful pull. Numerous innovations deliver unprecedented dynamics, further emphasized by the unmistakable, signature sound of the M engine.
